Privacy Policy & Legal Terms

At PixVas, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal data. This document outlines how we process your information when you use our image processing platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you use PixVas, we collect information that allows us to provide a high-quality experience. This collection happens in several ways:

  • check_circle Account Information: Name, email address, and authentication credentials when you sign up.
  • check_circle Usage Metadata: Information about how you interact with our image tools, processing times, and feature preferences.
  • check_circle Device Data: IP address, browser type, and operating system to ensure cross-platform compatibility.

Critical Data Note

PixVas does not claim ownership of the images you upload. Your source images are stored temporarily for processing and are automatically purged from our cache after 24 hours of inactivity.

4. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

To keep our basic tools free for everyone, we use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.

AdSense Compliance Details:

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to PixVas.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • We do not share your uploaded images or personal data with advertisers. Advertising is served based on browser-level metadata only.
