Frequency Asked Questions

Is this tool really free?

Yes, PixVas Vectorizer is 100% free forever. We don't require sign-ups, credits, or subscriptions.

What file formats can I upload?

You can upload any standard image format including JPG, PNG, and WEBP. The output will always be standard SVG code.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use local browser processing technology. Your images stay on your device and are never sent to a remote server.
